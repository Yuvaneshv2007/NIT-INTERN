5. MULTI-FACTOR REWARD SCORING CRITERIA
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
To ensure clinical safety and accuracy, each generated answer was graded out
of 1.00 using five separate objective metric weights:

Weight  Metric Name       Purpose / Measurement
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
35%     SBERT Similarity  Semantic similarity of the answer to ground truth.
25%     Faithfulness      Sentence-level check verifying that every claim is
                          strictly supported by retrieved textbook facts.
20%     ROUGE-L           Lexical N-gram overlap F1-measure.
10%     Speed Bonus       Linear latency penalty (0% at 300s, 100% at 0s).
10%     Context Relevancy SBERT similarity between query and retrieved chunks.
